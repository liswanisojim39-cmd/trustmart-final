Frequently Asked Questions
How do I post an item?
Click the "Sell" button at the bottom of your screen. Fill in your item's details, upload clear photos, and submit. It will go live immediately.
How do I stay safe?
Never send mobile money before seeing the item. Always meet in a public, well-lit place like a mall to inspect the goods before paying.
Can I edit my listing?
Yes, go to your Profile, click "My Listings," select the item, and tap the edit icon to update your price or photos.
